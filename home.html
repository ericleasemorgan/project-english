This is the home page of Project English, for the lack of a better title.

The goals of Project English are many-fold, listed in no priority order:

  • To provide useful information services to the University community
  • To amalgamate a number English language collections (EEBO, ECCO, and Sabin), into a coherent whole
  • To save time & money
  • To enable learners, teachers, and scholars to do "distant" and "scalable" reading against 10's of thousands of literary items
