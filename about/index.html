This page outlines and illustrates the content of Project English as well as each of its 3 sub-collections.

The whole collection

The whole collection is currently made up of approximately 62 thousand items, 8589 thousand pages, and 2 billion words. Which means, on average, each item is about 138 pages (or 46878 words) long. There are 29 distinct languages represented in the collection, but for all intents & purposes, English is the only language. Similarly, there are 1710 distinct cities of publication, but about 75% of them are either London, Boston, Dublin, Edinburgh, New York, or Philadelphia.
